Account Deletion Request

NextWave Music Sim

This page outlines the process for requesting deletion of your NextWave Music Sim account and all associated personal data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your game progress, songs, albums, collaborations, and achievements will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

üîê In-App Account Deletion

The fastest way to delete your account is directly through the app:

  1. Open NextWave Music Sim
  2. Navigate to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll to Account Actions section
  4. Tap DELETE ACCOUNT (red button)
  5. Confirm deletion in the dialog
  6. Enter your password for verification
  7. Your account and all data will be deleted immediately

üóëÔ∏è What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon successful verification of your deletion request, we will permanently delete:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, player profile
  • Game Progress: Artist stats, money, fame, fanbase, skill levels
  • Creative Content: All songs, albums, lyrics, and recordings
  • Social Data: Collaborations, relationships, chat messages
  • Community Content: EchoX posts, NexTube videos, comments
  • Notifications: All in-game notifications and news
  • Crew Memberships: Your association with any crews
  • Authentication Data: Firebase Authentication credentials

üìä Data Retention & Anonymization

Immediately Deleted (0-24 hours):

  • Personal identifiable information (name, email)
  • Private game data and creative content
  • Authentication credentials

Anonymized & Retained (up to 90 days):

For game integrity, anti-cheat measures, and leaderboard accuracy, the following data may be retained in anonymized form for up to 90 days:

  • Aggregate leaderboard statistics (no player identification)
  • Anonymous chart history (for NPC interactions)
  • Transaction logs (for fraud prevention)

Important: Your name, email, and user ID are removed from all retained data immediately. These records cannot be linked back to you after 90 days.

Never Retained:

  • Payment information (we don't process payments directly)
  • Private messages or chat logs (deleted immediately)
  • Avatar photos or custom images (deleted immediately)

This export includes all your songs, albums, stats, and game progress in a readable format.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, you cannot recover your data, progress, or username.

What if I want to start over with a new account?

After deletion, you're welcome to create a new account with the same or different email. However, your previous progress will not be restored.

Will other players see my content after deletion?

No. All your songs, albums, posts, and comments will be removed from the game. Collaborations you participated in will show your slot as "Deleted User."

What happens to crews I created?

If you're the only member, the crew is deleted. If other members exist, leadership transfers to another member automatically.

Do I need to uninstall the app?

No, but you can uninstall the app separately. Uninstalling does not delete your account data - you must explicitly request deletion.

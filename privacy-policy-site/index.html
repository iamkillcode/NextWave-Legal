1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

  • Google Account Information (if using Google Sign-In): Email address, display name, and profile picture
  • User ID: A unique identifier automatically generated by Firebase Authentication
  • Display Name: The artist name you choose for your in-game profile

1.2 Game Profile Data

To provide the game experience, we store:

  • Artist Statistics: Fame, money, energy, creativity, fanbase, loyal fanbase, skills (songwriting, lyrics, composition, experience, inspiration)
  • Career Information: Career start date, age, primary music genre, genre mastery levels (0-100%), unlocked genres
  • Verification Status: Whether you've applied for or received verified artist status
  • Location Data: Your in-game current region (e.g., USA, Europe, Asia, Africa, Latin America, Middle East, India, Oceania, Canada) - this is fictional game data, not real GPS location

1.3 Game Content You Create

We store content you create within the game:

  • Songs: Titles, genres, quality ratings, custom lyrics, release dates, streaming statistics, cover art, diss tracks, featured artists
  • Albums/EPs: Album information, track listings, sales data, certifications (Gold, Platinum, Diamond)
  • Social Media Posts: Posts you create on in-game EchoX social platform
  • NexTube Videos: Music videos and content uploaded to in-game NexTube platform
  • Chat Messages: Direct messages and conversations with other players via StarChat
  • Comments: Comments on songs, videos, and social posts
  • Avatar Customization: Your selected skin tone, hairstyle, facial features, clothing, and accessories
  • Bio/Profile: Biographical information you add to your artist profile

1.4 Gameplay Activity

To manage game progression and multiplayer features, we track:

  • Financial Transactions: In-game currency earned and spent (not real money), bank investments, loans
  • Tours and Concerts: Performance history, tour schedules, venue bookings, ticket sales
  • Collaborations: Partnerships with other players on songs and albums, royalty splits
  • Relationships: In-game friendships, romances, and rivalries with other players or NPCs
  • Beefs: Public feuds or rivalries initiated within the game
  • Side Hustles & Gigs: Part-time jobs (barista, rideshare, retail) and gig performances with rarity tiers
  • Crew Membership: Participation in player-created music crews, crew songs, crew challenges
  • Achievements: Certifications, chart positions, milestones, GigFinder streaks
  • Last Activity Date: When you last played to manage game time progression and fame decay
  • Last Release Date: When you last published content to prevent spam

1.5 Uploaded Media

When you upload custom content:

  • Cover Art Images: Custom album/song cover images uploaded via our image picker
  • Profile Pictures: Custom avatar images (if using custom upload feature)
  • Crew Symbols: Custom logos for music crews
  • Audio Files: Music recordings, vocal tracks, instrumentals, or other audio content you upload for your songs

These files are stored securely in Firebase Cloud Storage with unique identifiers and access controls. Audio files are processed and stored in compressed formats to optimize storage and streaming performance.

1.6 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Platform type (web, Android, iOS, Windows), browser type, screen resolution
  • Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage patterns, screen views, feature interactions, session duration, and app crashes to improve game performance
  • Authentication Tokens: Session tokens to keep you logged in securely
  • Server Timestamps: Game time synchronization data to ensure fair multiplayer gameplay (1 real hour = 1 in-game day)
  • Performance Metrics: App load times, network latency, Cloud Function execution times

1.7 Multiplayer & Social Data

To enable multiplayer features:

  • EchoX Social Media: Followers, following list, posts, likes, shares
  • Player Directory: Public profiles visible in searchable player directory
  • Global Charts: Your songs' performance on Hot 100, Spotlight 200, regional charts, weekly charts
  • Leaderboards: Rankings for top artists, most streamed songs, crew challenges
  • Reports: Reports you file about inappropriate content or behavior
  • Admin Gifts: Monetary gifts received from administrators

1.8 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Real-world GPS location or precise device location
  • Payment information (the game is free-to-play with in-game currency only)
  • Personal identification documents
  • Phone numbers (beyond what Google provides during sign-in)
  • Contact lists from your device
  • Information from other apps on your device
  • Biometric data
  • Health or medical information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for providing and improving the game experience:

2.1 Core Game Functionality

  • Maintain your artist profile and game progression

  • Enable multiplayer features (collaborations, chat, social media, beefs, crews)

  • Process in-game transactions, financial records, bank operations

  • Show your songs on global, regional, and weekly charts

  • Facilitate crew membership, crew songs, and crew challenges
  • Support relationship mechanics (friendships, romances, rivalries)
  • Enable beef/rivalry systems with diss tracks
  • Show NexTube music videos to other players

2.3 Game Balance & Anti-Cheat

  • Detect and prevent time manipulation or cheating using server timestamps

  • Ensure fair competition on leaderboards and charts

  • Validate client-side calculations with server-side verification

2.4 Analytics & Improvements

  • Analyze anonymous gameplay patterns to improve features
  • Monitor app performance, crash rates, and fix bugs
  • Understand which features are most popular
  • Optimize server performance and Firebase costs
  • A/B testing of new features via Firebase Remote Config
  • Telemetry for game balance adjustments

2.5 Administrative Functions

  • Provide customer support when needed
  • Respond to reports of inappropriate content or behavior
  • Enable admin review of verification applications

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Where We Store Data

Your data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore and Firebase Cloud Storage, which are hosted on Google Cloud Platform servers:

  • Primary Region: us-central1 (Iowa, USA)
  • Secondary Region: us-east1 (South Carolina, USA)
  • Storage: Firebase Cloud Storage for uploaded images and audio

Data may be replicated across multiple data centers for redundancy and performance.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS 1.2+ encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Firebase Cloud Firestore and Cloud Storage encrypt all data at rest using AES-256
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication with secure session tokens and token refresh mechanisms
  • Access Controls: Firestore security rules restrict data access based on user identity and role
  • Input Validation: Server-side validation and sanitization prevents injection attacks and data corruption
  • Rate Limiting: Cloud Functions implement rate limiting to prevent DDoS and abuse
  • Monitoring: Automated monitoring for suspicious activity, unusual patterns, and security breaches
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security audits and dependency updates
  • Principle of Least Privilege: Services only have access to data they need

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained indefinitely as long as you use the game
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for 365+ days may be flagged for review; we'll attempt to notify you before deletion
  • Deleted Accounts: Upon account deletion request, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Legal Retention: Some data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud investigation)
  • Backups: Deleted data is removed from backups within 90 days

4.4 Your Responsibility

To help protect your account:

  • Keep your Google account credentials secure
  • Log out from shared devices
  • Report suspicious activity to us immediately
  • Do not share your account with others
